Welcome to TCS Website

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is an Indian multinational company that offers IT services, consulting, and business solutions.

TCS has been partnering with some of the world's largest businesses for over 55 years and has over 614,000 consultants in 55 countries. TCS is part of the Tata Group, India's largest multinational business group

Tata Consultancy Services Limited, initially started as Tata Computer Systems, was founded in 1968 by a division of Tata Sons Limited.

Its early contracts included punched card services to sister company TISCO (now Tata Steel), working on an Inter-Branch Reconciliation System for the Central Bank of India,[24] and providing bureau services to Unit Trust of India.

Former CEOs of TCS

  • F. C. Kohli, co-founder and first CEO and MD of TCS from 1968 to 1996, known as "Father of Indian IT Industry"
  • Subramaniam Ramadorai, CEO and MD of TCS from 1996 to 2009, and Vice - Chairman till 6 October 2014
  • Natarajan Chandrasekaran, CEO, Executive Director, and CEO of TCS from 2009 to 2017
  • Rajesh Gopinathan,CEO and MD of TCS from 2017 to May 2023
  • K. Krithivasan, CEO and MD of TCS from June 1 2023
